Temple Circuit Itinerary

Kashmir is the birthplace of Kashmir Shaivism. These ancient temples span over 2,000 years of faith, history, and breathtaking architecture.

1

Arrive Srinagar

Shankaracharya Temple darshan on Gopadri Hill. Panoramic views of Dal Lake. Evening shikara ride at sunset.

Srinagar City
2

Srinagar Temples

Sharika Devi Temple at Hari Parbat, Ranbireshwar Temple (12 Jyotirlingas), Jyeshtha Devi Temple, and Hazratbal Shrine.

Old Srinagar Circuit
3

Kheer Bhawani

Kheer Bhawani Temple at Tullamulla (mystical color-changing spring). Manasbal Lake & Sinking Shiva Temple.

Ganderbal District
4

Pahalgam

Mamleshwar Shiva Temple on Lidder River. Betaab Valley. Baisaran meadow. Gateway to Amarnath Yatra.

Pahalgam, Anantnag
5

Martand Sun Temple

Martand Sun Temple ruins (7th century CE, UNESCO tentative list). Awantiswami Temple at Awantipora. Mattan sacred pond.

South Kashmir Heritage Trail
6

Sonmarg & Departure

Sonmarg glacier excursion. Thajiwas Glacier trek. Kashmir shopping — shawls, saffron, dry fruits. Departure.

Sonmarg, Srinagar
