About This Package

The Kashmir Valley Explorer is our most comprehensive and beloved package — crafted for travelers who want to experience the full magic of Jammu & Kashmir in one seamless journey. Over 8 days, you'll float on Dal Lake's mystical waters, seek blessings at ancient Hindu temples, ride Asia's highest gondola in Gulmarg, trek through the meadows of Pahalgam, and stand before the Thajiwas glacier in Sonmarg.

Kashmir is not just a destination — it's a spiritual experience. Our expert local guides will take you beyond the tourist trail to discover hidden temples, share stories of Kashmir Shaivism, and introduce you to the warmth of Kashmiri hospitality through authentic wazwan cuisine and chai by the chinar trees.

This package is ideal for families, couples, and small groups seeking the perfect blend of pilgrimage, nature, and culture. Every detail — from airport pickup to the last shikara ride — is personally managed by our Srinagar-based team.

Day-by-Day Itinerary

1

Arrival in Srinagar + Dal Lake

Day 1

Arrive at Srinagar Airport — airport pickup by our representative

Check-in to iconic Dal Lake houseboat (Deluxe category)

Srinagar Temple Circuit

Day 2

Sunrise shikara ride on Dal Lake (6:30 AM)

Shankaracharya Temple — hilltop darshan with panoramic views

Sharika Devi Temple at Hari Parbat & Ranbireshwar Temple (12 Jyotirlingas)

Jyeshtha Devi Temple — ancient wooden shrine by water

Hazratbal Shrine — Kashmir's most revered mosque

Snow activities: skiing / snowboarding / snow walk

Baba Reshi Shrine visit on the way back
